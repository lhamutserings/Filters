Instagram and Facebook Filters

PyDataNYC filters are now available on Facebook and Instagram effects under 'PyDataNYC'. Make your conference experience extra memorable with a photo or two!  

Here's how to access these filters: Click the + button at the top left corner on Ig or Fb. Use photos in your existing library or select 'Camera' to take a new photo/video. Swipe all the way to the right through the filters icons at the bottom of the screen, then tap the magnifying glass icon (Browse Effects). Search 'PyDataNYC' and tap the magnifying glass. Choose your filter(s) and have fun!
